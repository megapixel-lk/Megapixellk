About

Design with
purpose

Megapixellk is a creative design studio based in Sri Lanka, specialising in visual communication that blends aesthetic precision with strategic intent. From brand identities to immersive motion work, every project begins with understanding your story — and ends with an image that tells it better than words.

With expertise across print, digital, AI-powered imagery, and video, we bridge the traditional and the emerging — crafting visuals that feel both timeless and contemporary.

Privacy
Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Information We Collect

When you contact us through this website, we collect only the information you voluntarily provide — your name, email address, and project details. We do not collect any information automatically beyond standard server logs.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to respond to your enquiry and, if agreed upon, to facilitate design projects. We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond direct communication with you.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains confidential and is only accessible to Megapixellk for the purposes stated above.

Data Retention

We retain contact information only for as long as necessary to complete your project and any follow-up communication. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. Any cookies present are strictly necessary for basic site functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@megapixellk.com and we will respond within 7 business days.

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, reach us at hello@megapixellk.com
