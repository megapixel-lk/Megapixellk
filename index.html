Megapixellk is a creative design studio based in Sri Lanka, specialising in visual communication that blends aesthetic precision with strategic intent. From brand identities to immersive motion work, every project begins with understanding your story — and ends with an image that tells it better than words.

With expertise across print, digital, AI-powered imagery, and video, we bridge the traditional and the emerging — crafting visuals that feel both timeless and contemporary.
